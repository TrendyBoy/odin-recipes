Rice

A must have for bodybuilders and asian food enthusiasts everywhere!

Ingredients!
  • Rice
  • Water
  • A pot

Steps!
  1. Rinse the rice.
  2. Use the right ratio of water.
  3. Bring the water to a boil.Once it's boiling, add a big pinch of salt.
  4. Maintain a simmer.
  5. Cook without peeking or stirring.
  6. Let the rice rest covered.
  7. Fluff the rice with a fork
